Reality Checks By Barry Minkin

In his book "REALITY CHECKS," Barry Minkin discusses about the divisive nature of diversity, which he argues has been distorted into a tool to sow discord among Americans. He highlights how groups like Black Lives Matter and Antifa exploit the concept of diversity to justify violence and undermine authority. Minkin also points out a perceived two-tiered justice system, where BLM rioters are treated leniently compared to those involved in the January 6th Capitol riot. He criticizes the media for bias and the education system for indoctrinating students with left-wing ideology. Ultimately, Minkin calls for action to combat these threats and preserve America's unity and culture.

Barry Minkin

With over five decades of experience in global management consulting, Barry Minkin has worked closely with organizations and government entities to anticipate future opportunities and challenges. During his time at Stanford Research Institute (SRI International), he published two influential books, “Future In Sight” and “Econoquake,” which accurately predicted key trends and economic shifts.

As an Organizational Psychologist, he excels at separating fact from speculation and promoting critical thinking in a world filled with misinformation. Drawing on historical examples like Joseph Goebbels’ propaganda tactics, he emphasizes the dangers of distorted narratives. In his latest book, “Brainwashed! America’s Cultural Revolution,” he gets deeper into topics like political polarization and global conflicts, challenging conventional wisdom and advocating for critical inquiry. By analyzing current events and revisiting past predictions, he offers a unique perspective on our society’s trajectory and encourages thoughtful discussions about the future.
